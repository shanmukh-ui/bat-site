Batman — Biography

Batman is a vigilante who protects Gotham City using intelligence, discipline, and preparation rather than superpowers. After witnessing crime at a young age, he dedicated his life to mastering combat, strategy, and technology.

Operating from the shadows, he strikes fear into criminals while standing as a symbol of control and justice. His strength is not physical power alone, but an unbreakable will and a strict code against killing.
